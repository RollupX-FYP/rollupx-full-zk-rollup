\subsection{Project Phases}
\vspace{12pt}
The project is structured into six key phases, each with specific objectives and deliverables. The phases are:
\begin{enumerate}
    \item \textbf{Project Initiation and Research} (Completed): Identified the research problem, defined project scope and objectives, and conducted a comprehensive literature review.
    \item \textbf{System Architecture Design} (Completed): Defined the modular system architecture, including sequencer, executor, prover, and data availability components, and planned experimental parameters.
    \item \textbf{Prototype Implementation} (Completed): Developed the modular ZK-Rollup framework with configurable batching, sequencing, and DA strategies, and successfully deployed on an Ethereum testnet.
    \item \textbf{Conduct Experiments, Benchmarking and Optimization} (In Progress): Establishing benchmarking metrics, conducting controlled experiments with various configurations, and performing optimization.
    \item \textbf{Data Analysis} (Planned): Perform statistical analysis of experimental results and validate findings against theoretical predictions.
    \item \textbf{Documentation and Presentation} (Planned): Prepare the final documentation, research paper, and present the project results.
\end{enumerate}

The project remains on track for successful completion within the planned timeframe. The modular architecture and configurable design have proven effective for controlled experimentation, positioning the team well for the critical benchmarking and analysis phases ahead.
