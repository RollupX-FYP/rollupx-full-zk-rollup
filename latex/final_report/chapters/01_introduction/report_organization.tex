\section{Document Structure}
\label{sec:document_structure}

The remainder of this report is structured as follows. 
Chapter \ref{ch:background} provides a detailed background on blockchain scalability concepts, the scalability trilemma, and ZK-Rollups. 
Chapter \ref{ch:literature_review} reviews relevant literature across broad scalability taxonomies and focused ZK-rollup bottlenecks. 
Chapter \ref{ch:problem_objectives} outlines the refined problem statement, research questions, and objectives. 
Chapter \ref{ch:system_architecture} presents the high-level system architecture and component interactions. 
Chapter \ref{ch:methodology} discusses the research methodology and evaluation metrics. 
Chapter \ref{ch:implementation} delves deeply into the actual implementation of the prototype's core components (Sequencer, Executor, Submitter, and Smart Contracts). 
Chapter \ref{ch:experimental_setup} outlines the containerized testing environment and benchmark configurations, followed by Chapter \ref{ch:results} which presents the findings. 
Chapter \ref{ch:discussion} provides an in-depth analysis of the throughput--latency--cost trade-offs observed during benchmarking. 
Chapter \ref{ch:threats_to_validity} addresses potential threats to the validity of the results. 
Chapter \ref{ch:individual_contributions} documents project management and the team's individual contributions. 
Finally, Chapter \ref{ch:conclusion} concludes the report and suggests directions for future work.
