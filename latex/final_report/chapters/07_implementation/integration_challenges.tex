\section{Integration Challenges}
\label{sec:integration_challenges}
During implementation, several integration challenges were encountered and resolved. The shift to a modular architecture required robust communication protocols, leading to the use of gRPC and Protocol Buffers between the Sequencer, Executor, and Submitter. State synchronization between the Sequencer's pessimistic cache and the Executor's canonical state required careful handling to prevent race conditions. Furthermore, managing the volatile local Hardhat RPC required implementing robust retry logic and accurate gas estimation within the Submitter's Saga engine. Dependency conflicts, particularly between the \texttt{nightly-2024-08-01} Rust toolchain and \texttt{edition2024} crates in the Executor, required specific workarounds during the build process.
