\subsection{The Blockchain Quadrilemma}
Some recent literature expands the trilemma into a \textit{quadrilemma} by introducing a fourth dimension: \textbf{Trust} or \textbf{Privacy}. In these models, attempting to scale the network via certain L2 solutions (like Validiums or sidechains) may compromise trustless operation, requiring users to place trust in external Data Availability committees or operators. ZK-Rollups aim to preserve the trustless nature of the base layer while significantly enhancing scalability, thereby providing a more balanced solution to the quadrilemma.
