\section{Research Questions}
\label{sec:research_questions}
To address this problem, the research is guided by the following primary research questions:

\begin{enumerate}
    \item \textbf{RQ1:} How can a modular ZK-rollup benchmarking prototype be designed to support controlled experimentation over sequencing, batching, execution, proof handling, submission, and data availability?
    \item \textbf{RQ2:} How do batch size/frequency, sequencing policy, and DA mode affect throughput, latency, finalization time, DA footprint, and cost per transaction?
    \item \textbf{RQ3:} How can benchmark-mode proof-delay or mock-prover modeling help isolate prover-stage effects from sequencing, execution, and Layer-1 submission overhead?
    \item \textbf{RQ4:} What practical bottlenecks and implementation trade-offs appear when building and benchmarking a modular ZK-rollup research prototype?
\end{enumerate}
