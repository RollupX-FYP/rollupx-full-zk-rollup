This chapter presents the findings from the automated benchmark runs. It is important to emphasize that these results are derived from a \textit{controlled benchmarking prototype} using synthetic workloads and, in some cases, parameterized mock-prover delays. Therefore, the absolute numbers (such as maximum TPS) represent a configuration-level, relative comparison rather than a claim of production mainnet performance.
