\subsection{Cost Amortization}
As expected, larger batch sizes significantly reduced the amortized Layer-1 gas cost per transaction. Because the L1 smart contract incurs a fixed base cost for verifying the state root and DA commitment, spreading this cost over 500 transactions makes the rollup much cheaper for the end user compared to batches of 10.
