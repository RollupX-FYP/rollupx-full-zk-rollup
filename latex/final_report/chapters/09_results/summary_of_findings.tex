\section{Summary of Findings}
\label{sec:summary_of_findings}
The observations clearly define the fundamental throughput--latency--cost trade-off inherent in rollup batching policies. Furthermore, the DA footprint remains the most significant L1 cost factor, and proof generation delay represents the primary bottleneck to hard finality.

\textit{Note: The dynamic batching cost model referenced in early literature is analytical and not implemented in the current runtime codebase. The results presented here reflect the empirical behavior of the implemented microservice stack.}
