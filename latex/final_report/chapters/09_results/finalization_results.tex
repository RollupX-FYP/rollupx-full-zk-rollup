\subsection{Finalization Time (Hard Finality)}
The proof delay heavily dominated the batch finalization time. When the delay was set to 0s, the system was bottlenecked primarily by local database I/O and L1 submission limits. As the delay increased to 30s, the time gap between soft finality (batch sealed) and hard finality (L1 verification) widened dramatically.
