\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=1pt,itemsep=0.25em,topsep=0pt]
    \item \textbf{Experimental Framework:} The prototype provides a controlled environment for studying how batching, sequencing, and DA choices shift throughput, latency, and cost trade-offs.
    \item \textbf{Key Finding:} Preliminary synthetic benchmarks show that blob-based DA reduces cost per transaction compared to calldata, highlighting the impact of EIP-4844-style data posting.
    \item \textbf{Future Work:} Integrate full cryptographic prover benchmarking, extend evaluation with trace-driven workloads, and explore decentralized sequencing.
